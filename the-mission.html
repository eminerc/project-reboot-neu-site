Our Mission Events Fuel the Movement

Let’s be honest,

we have all scrolled ig reels on the toilet until our legs went numb.

Social media platforms are built to exploit our psychology, hijack our attention, and keep us scrolling instead of living.

We didn’t stumble into this. We were engineered into it.

But we see it now.
And we can change the narrative.

ProjectRebootNEU is a social movement.

It is the separation of our free time from the grip of addictive algorithms.

It is social events with no feeds. no notifications. no digital noise.

It is being present with the people around you.

The most valuable thing about being here isn’t the degree.

It’s each other.

We are not anti-social media.

We are tech-intentional.

Scroll less. Live more.
